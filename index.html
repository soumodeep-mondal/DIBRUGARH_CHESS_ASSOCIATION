WELCOME TO Dibrugarh District Chess Association (DDCA)

Registrar under society Act XXI of 1860RS/DIB/255/C/78

The Dibrugarh District Chess Association (DDCA) is an affiliated district unit of the All Assam Chess Association (AACA). DDCA is part of the AACA network, which aims to promote and organize chess activities at the district level.

Our goal is to teach children in grades K-12 abstract concepts of planning, analysis and strategy through the game of chess. Kids will be excited to understand and amazed by the positive experience that chess offers.

DDCA focuses on organizing chess tournaments, events, and activities within Dibrugarh district.
